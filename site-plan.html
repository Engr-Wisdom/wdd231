Site Name

Cara Ecommerce Website

The website is an ecommerce platform for shoppers and product enthusiasts. It offers quality products, detailed descriptions, and a seamless shopping experience with trusted brands and great deals.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Cara Ecommerce website is to provide an engaging online platform for users to browse, discover, and purchase products. It aims to deliver a user-friendly shopping experience by showcasing products dynamically, allowing users to filter, add items to a cart, and complete purchases easily. Additionally, it provides a modern, visually appealing interface with responsive design and interactive elements to enhance customer satisfaction and encourage repeat visits.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #e3e6f3 (Light Lavender/Blue) - Used for page backgrounds and section backgrounds
  • Secondary Color: #088178 (Teal) - Used for buttons, links, and highlights
  • Accent Color 1: #1a1a1a (Dark Gray/Black) - Used for headings, main text, and icons
  • Accent Color 2: #ffffff (White) - Used for text on dark backgrounds, cards, and overlays

Typography

  • Heading Font: Arial
  • Body Font: Open Sans
